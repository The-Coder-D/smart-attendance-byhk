Smart Attendance System

Modernizing campus management through technology.

Our Mission

To eliminate manual roll calls and streamline academic workflows. This system provides a seamless experience for faculty to track attendance via dynamic QR codes, while offering students a centralized hub to monitor their academic performance and access study materials.

Architecture Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend Engine: Node.js & Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB (MERN Integration)
  • Data Vis: Chart.js
